## 5. Try It Out!

### Draw a Letter
1. Press **A** for alphabet mode
2. Point your **index finger** up
3. Draw a letter in the air
4. Press **R** to recognize
5. Press **ENTER** to search on Google

### Draw a Shape
1. Hold up **2 fingers** (index + middle)
2. Move to create shape preview
3. Point **1 finger** to draw it

### Change Color
1. Hold up **3 fingers**
2. Move hand to top color bar
3. Select a color

## Common Issues
**Hand not detected?**
- Improve lighting
- Keep hand in camera view
- Try moving closer/farther
